About Us

Hennastoreproject, founded by Engineer Rahma Fares, is a unique shopping platform dedicated to showcasing traditional Palestinian cultural dresses. The project's goal is to promote and preserve the rich heritage of Palestinian women's fashion. Targeting females of all ages, the store offers a variety of traditional dresses and accessories, allowing customers to purchase complete outfits that reflect Palestinian cultural significance. Unlike typical fashion stores, Hennastoreproject focuses specifically on Palestinian cultural dresses, positioning itself as a cultural project. Built using Django, the platform provides a seamless shopping experience. Inspired by Rahma's childhood experience with Palestinian dresses, the project aims to expand its offerings to include traditional men's clothing, further enriching its cultural showcase. Customers can access Hennastoreproject through a dedicated link and Instagram page.
